Classify U.S. land ownership into one of these:

1 Federal
2 State
3 Local
4 Corporate
5 Conservation
6 Association
7 Family

Use OWN1 and OWN2 only. Return a single digit.

Rules (compact and corrective):

FEDERAL (1):
Clear federal agencies only:
USFS, USDA, BLM, NPS, USFWS, DOI, Army Corps.
“USA/American/National” does NOT imply federal if business terms appear.

STATE (2):
Requires a real state name or “State of <State>”.
DNR, DOT, State Parks only count if tied to a real state name.
“Department of ___” alone is not state.
Water or resource departments → not automatically state.

LOCAL (3):
City, Town, County, Borough, Parish, Village, Municipality.
Fire/Police/Sheriff/School District/Road & Bridge.
Water/Sewer/Irrigation/Sanitation districts → Local.

CORPORATE (4):
Any business suffix: LLC, Inc, Corp, Co, LP, LLP, Holdings, Partners, Group, Solutions, Industries.
Business suffix overrides patriotic or government-like wording.

CONSERVATION (5):
Conservancy, Conservation, Alliance, Council, Habitat, Restoration, Land Trust (nonprofit sense).

ASSOCIATION (6):
Club, Association, Fellowship, Society, Congregation, Church.

FAMILY (7):
Family Trust, Estate, Heirs, Ranch, Farm (non-commercial), surnames without business suffixes.

Resolution:
Federal → State → Local → Corporate → Conservation → Association → Family.
